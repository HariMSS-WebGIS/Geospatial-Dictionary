# 🛠️ Geospatial Dictionary: Administrator Moderation & GUI Management Manual

This guide explains how to access, moderate, and manage geospatial terms directly from the graphical user interface (GUI) of the Geospatial Dictionary application.

---

## 🔗 Live Site Links

> [!NOTE]
> * **Live Website**: [https://harimss-webgis.github.io/Geospatial-Dictionary/](https://harimss-webgis.github.io/Geospatial-Dictionary/)
> * **Admin Access URL**: [https://harimss-webgis.github.io/Geospatial-Dictionary/?admin=true](https://harimss-webgis.github.io/Geospatial-Dictionary/?admin=true)

---

## 🔑 1. How to Enable Administrator Mode

To unlock administrative rights and view moderation controls on the live website:

1. Open your internet browser and navigate to the live website.
2. In the browser address bar, append **`?admin=true`** to the end of the URL and press `Enter`.
3. The page will reload in Admin Mode. You will verify this by seeing a cyan-colored **"Moderation Panel"** button appear in the top header next to the "+ Add Term" button.

---

## ✍️ 2. Submitting Terms as an Administrator

When you are in Administrator Mode, terms you contribute are marked with distinct priority tags:

* **Admin Custom Badge**: When you fill out the "+ Add Term" form and save a term, it is automatically marked with a blue **Admin Custom** badge on the site.
* **Overriding Guest Submissions**: Terms added by standard visitors are labeled with an orange **Guest Contribution** badge. As an administrator, you can view, edit, or override these entries.

---

## 📊 3. Using the Moderation Dashboard Panel

Clicking the cyan **"Moderation Panel"** button in the header opens a dashboard that compiles all submitted terms chronologically:

### Chronological Groupings:
* 🔴 **Added Today**: Contributions submitted within the current 24-hour cycle.
* 🟡 **Added This Week (Last 7 Days)**: Submissions added during the last week.
* 🔵 **Added Earlier**: Older terms stored in the database.

### Dashboard Actions:
* **"View" Button**: Click this next to any term in the panel list. The dashboard will automatically close, scroll to the selected term card on the homepage, and open its details drawer.
* **"Delete" Button**: Click this next to any term to remove it immediately. The system will prompt you for confirmation and then purge the term from the database.

---

## 🗑️ 4. Deleting Terms Directly from the Home Screen

In Administrator Mode, you can moderate terms directly from the glossary card feed without opening the dashboard:

1. Browse or search for any term card on the homepage.
2. Notice the **Trash Can icon** visible in the top-right corner of the term card (this icon is completely hidden from standard website visitors).
3. Click the **Trash Can icon**.
4. Confirm the deletion popup dialog, and the term card will disappear instantly from the site.
